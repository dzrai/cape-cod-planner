Day by day

Day 1 · Thu Aug 20 — Arrival via the pretty way

Morning Leave NYC by ~8am; over the bridge by early afternoon.
Afternoon Enter on Route 6A through Sandwich — Heritage Museums & Gardens or the Glass Museum, antique-shop east.
Evening Check into Chatham; easy Main Street dinner; sunset walk to Chatham Lighthouse.
Arrival day stays light on purpose.
💵 Est. day cost for two: ~$240 — gas/tolls $50 · lunch en route $40 · Heritage Gardens $50 · dinner $100

Day 2 · Fri Aug 21 — Chatham, properly

Morning Inn breakfast, Main Street stroll — shops, galleries, coffee, Candy Manor.
Afternoon Fish pier seals around midday; beach hours at Lighthouse Beach (or the seal cruise).
Evening Aplaya Kitchen & Tiki Bar — the fun dinner.
inn / clam-shack lunch / Aplaya
💵 Est. day cost for two: ~$200 — coffee + Candy Manor $20 · pier lunch $60 · Aplaya $120 · beach $0  (+$90 if you add the seal cruise)

Day 3 · Sat Aug 22 — Provincetown day

Morning Drive up (~1 hr) with the Marconi overlook stop in Wellfleet.
Afternoon Commercial Street galleries + shops, Pilgrim Monument museum, Portuguese Bakery malasadas.
Evening Early dinner at The Canteen (casual) or Ceraldi (tasting, book ahead) → drive back.
Park once (Grace Hall lot) and walk everything. Saturday in P-town is lively — arrive by 10am to park easily.
💵 Est. day cost for two: ~$160 — gas $15 · parking $15 · malasadas $15 · Pilgrim Monument $45 · The Canteen $70  (Ceraldi swap: +$150)

Day 4 · Sun Aug 23 — Outer Cape slow day + the splurge

Morning National Seashore — Nauset Light + Coast Guard Beach walk (~$25 parking covers the day).
Afternoon Wellfleet: gallery loop, harbor oysters at Mac's, Uncle Tim's Bridge. Porch hour back at the inn.
Evening Twenty-Eight Atlantic at Wequassett — the celebration dinner.
inn / oysters in Wellfleet / Twenty-Eight Atlantic
💵 Est. day cost for two: ~$325 — Seashore parking $25 · Wellfleet oysters $50 · Twenty-Eight Atlantic $250

Day 5 · Mon Aug 24 — Departure

Morning Checkout ~11am; one last beach walk or Main Street coffee.
Afternoon Monday exodus is gentle — home by evening with four full days before the wedding. Optional lunch stop in Falmouth or Sandwich.
💵 Est. day cost for two: ~$115 — coffee $15 · lunch stop $50 · gas/tolls $50
